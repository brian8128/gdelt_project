Description of Principle Components

For my project we will have principle components of market movements as the market indicators rather than the so called 'big 4' tech companies.

Error Term

It may also be interesting the movement of the market that cannot be explained by the top k principle components. Here we discuss the error term. Exactly how to do this is an open question but we want to list 'important' changes that can't be explained by the principle components. This should look at the total market cap of companies in addition to their percent change because it's not that important if we can't explain why some tiny startups price changed.
